The keyboard is equipped with Jwick Ultimate Black switches. These switches boast new molds and incorporate special additives in both the top and bottom housing materials, meticulously designed to enhance both the bottoming out and topping out sounds. The Jwick Ultimate offers a distinctive 'thocky' sound profile, delivering a deep and satisfying auditory experience. Renowned for their durability and exceptional quality, Jwick switches strike a balance between affordability and performance, making them an excellent choice for users seeking a tactile switch with a pronounced and satisfying feedback during typing sessions.

The AKKO 5075, a gasket mount keyboard kit with a wired version and knob, incorporates a sandwiched silicone pad in the gasket mount, serving as a cushion to absorb shock and modify the typing sound profile. Additionally, it offers the option for a PE foam mod to further customize acoustics, complemented by customizable RGB backlighting and side LEDs for a visually stunning keyboard experience.

Enhance the aesthetic appeal of your keyboard with our exclusive artisan keycaps, meticulously crafted from glossy resin. These keycaps feature a unique swirly design inside, reminiscent of a rich and captivating flesh-like texture. The glossy finish adds a premium touch, and the keycaps are designed to beautifully shine through with backlighting, creating a truly customized and visually stunning keyboard experience. Each keycap is a work of art, adding a touch of individuality to your keyboard setup.
